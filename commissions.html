Golden Affiliates Commissions



At GoldenAffiliates we pay commissions based on a revenue share model. Commissions are calculated on the total net losses your players generate within our Sportsbook, Casino, Racebook, and Lottery games. For example if your players generate $1,000 in net gaming revenue during any given week, you would earn at a 25% revenue share percentage level ($1,000 X .25) a total of $250 in commissions.

We pay commissions dirrectly to your affiliate account on a weekly basis, unlike many other - standard programs which award commissions on a 30 day basis. Your also welcome to wager your commissions anytime or request a withdrawel via Person to Person transfer, Moneybookers, or Neteller. Your commissions will continue on for the life of the player accounts as long as you remain an active affiliate of GoldenAffiliates.

We welcome all portal owners to contact us for more information regarding their revenue share commission structure. In order to get started simply register your site details and payment information. Within the next 24 hours your application will be reviewed and approved for affiliate access. On average we approve nearly every application (9 out of 10) and start out entry level portal owners at 20-25% rev share.

High traffic sites and targeted iGaming portals are generally set up at a higher commission level 30% and up. We look forward to working with you and forming a lucrative campaign.
